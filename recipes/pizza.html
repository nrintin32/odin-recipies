Garlic Pizza Toast

Description

Easy Breezy Garlic Pizza Toast is a quick and simple recipe that combines the flavors of garlic bread and pizza into a delightful snack or appetizer. Sargento® Shredded Mozzarella Natural Cheese is melted and bubbly, creating a deliciously crispy and cheesy garlic pizza toast, a convenient and tasty option for those craving the flavors of pizza in a fuss-free manner.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices sourdough bread
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 Tbsp garlic salt
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 pkg. Sargento® Shredded Mozzarella Natural Cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning
