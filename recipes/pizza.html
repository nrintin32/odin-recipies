Garlic Pizza Toast

Description

Easy Breezy Garlic Pizza Toast is a quick and simple recipe that combines the flavors of garlic bread and pizza into a delightful snack or appetizer. Sargento® Shredded Mozzarella Natural Cheese is melted and bubbly, creating a deliciously crispy and cheesy garlic pizza toast, a convenient and tasty option for those craving the flavors of pizza in a fuss-free manner.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices sourdough bread
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 Tbsp garlic salt
  • 1/2 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 pkg. Sargento® Shredded Mozzarella Natural Cheese
  • 1/2 cup pepperoni
  • 1 Tbsp Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Heat air fryer to 360 degrees F.
  2. Spread butter on bread and sprinkle garlic salt over butter on bread. Add two tablespoons of marinara over garlic bread. Sprinkle two tablespoons of shredded mozzarella cheese. Sprinkle Italian seasoning on top of the cheese. Layer three slices of assembled bread into air fryer basket.
  3. Cook at 360 degrees F in air fryer between 5-7 mins until light golden brown. Enjoy!
